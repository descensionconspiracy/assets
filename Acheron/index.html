MERELY A BORROWED NAME. GALAXY RANGER, ACHERON... those are the names she is now called. She is but a wanderer without origin and name, traversing the cosmos with nothing but her blade in hand. Though aloof and taciturn, her blade flicks out like lashing lightning. And yet, she always strikes with her scabbard, never drawing the sword free.

WE DO NOT CONVINCE ALL TO CHOOSE THE RIGHT PATH—BUT TO ACTIVELY SAVE THEMSELVES. In her travels, she has faced and witnessed countless setbacks, and many more tragedies; the glory and disgrace of several planets and civilizations.

But ACHERON is no hero: she does not actively seek to interfere in the matters she comes upon—rather dealing with them carrying a stark indifference, as is the way of NIHILITY. Selfish in her conduct, she will only act when a matter holds particular significance to her—preferring, instead, to subtly inspire and encourage those around her to seek salvation through their own means.

PEOPLE DON'T CHOOSE THEIR FATE—FATE CHOOSES THEM. ACHERON knows she has nowhere to come back to: though the past forges who we are, it is but a fleeting thing—as with everything, it too must cease to be.

Occasionally, she comes across one with whom she may share her travels—in the appreciation of the journey—but, in the end, she is and will always be ALONE. She does not resent the cards fate has dealt to her, nor does she wail in lamentation, for it is precisely her inability to return, to retrace her own steps, that allows her to wander forward without fear, towards that primordial light under the veil of NIHILITY...

FIGHTING STYLE. ACHERON is well-versed both in swordplay and martial arts—preferring practicality over power. Her fighting style and strategies are more suited for disarming and neutralizing opponents, rather than outright killing them; this is simply because she finds it more convenient not to be in the spotlight, though some have attributed it due to a kinder nature within the Emanator.

EXTRASENSORY PERCEPTION AND INTERACTION. ACHERON is capable of doing the impossible and seeing the invisible, capable of seeing souls due to her connection to NIHILITY; one of her responsibilities is guiding souls within the ACHERON RIVER.

IMMORTALITY. Being a Self-Annihilator, the concept of "aging" holds no ability over her; and death does not come easy, as well, being capable of reattaching limbs naturally if they are severed, though not without cost to her present stamina, nor is it instantaneous.

DIMENSIONAL TRAVEL. Emanators are capable of crossing the boundaries between worlds and imaginary spaces.

LIGHTNING & ENERGY SOVEREIGNTY. Owing to her previous experiences commanding Lightning through the third Edict Edge, ACHERON has the ability to control Electricity and project energy, particularly through her slashes—well, scabbard strikes. This extends itself to electromagnetic field manipulation, but only if a certain focus is given to it—she hasn't felt the need to practice this particular ability as of late.

NIHILITY. Among the Emanators, the Emanator of Nihility is an unique existence. As its Aeon, by default, does not care about anything, for it is NIHILITY, she is capable of invoking its power almost endlessly when she draws her sword. In this state, Acheron's hair becomes completely white, and crimson tattoos run over her flesh.

With this ability, the Emanator can bring time to a standstill, cut through space, real or imaginary—such as dreams—and bring anything and anybody affected by her blade to the Horizon of Existence, where she can choose to wield NIHILITY to completely erase the fact of their existence, effectively making it so they never existed at all.

This condition affects her negatively; consumed by NIHILITY as she is, her past does not exist, in the sense that it was erased from space-time: it is as if she simply 'appeared in the universe' one day, though she can recall such erased memories when drawing upon her blade.
