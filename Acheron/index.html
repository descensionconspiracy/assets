MERELY A BORROWED NAME. GALAXY RANGER, ACHERON... those are the names she is now called. She is but a wanderer without origin and name, traversing the cosmos with nothing but her blade in hand. Though aloof and taciturn, her blade flicks out like lashing lightning. And yet, she always strikes with her scabbard, never drawing the sword free.

WE DO NOT CONVINCE ALL TO CHOOSE THE RIGHT PATH—BUT TO ACTIVELY SAVE THEMSELVES. In her travels, she has faced and witnessed countless setbacks, and many more tragedies; the glory and disgrace of several planets and civilizations.

But ACHERON is no hero: she does not actively seek to interfere in the matters she comes upon—rather dealing with them carrying a stark indifference, as is the way of NIHILITY. Selfish in her conduct, she will only act when a matter holds particular significance to her—preferring, instead, to subtly inspire and encourage those around her to seek salvation through their own means.

PEOPLE DON'T CHOOSE THEIR FATE—FATE CHOOSES THEM. ACHERON knows she has nowhere to come back to: though the past forges who we are, it is but a fleeting thing—as with everything, it too must cease to be.

Occasionally, she comes across one with whom she may share her travels—in the appreciation of the journey—but, in the end, she is and will always be ALONE. She does not resent the cards fate has dealt to her, nor does she wail in lamentation, for it is precisely her inability to return, to retrace her own steps, that allows her to wander forward without fear, towards that primordial light under the veil of NIHILITY...
